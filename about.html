About SyncNote

SyncNote is a modern, lightweight platform designed for writing notes, uploading files, and sharing information instantly. It allows users to create their own workspace simply by entering a unique name, making collaboration and quick access easy.

What You Can Do

With SyncNote, you can:

  • Create and edit notes in real-time
  • Upload and preview files (PDFs, images, documents)
  • Access your workspace from anywhere
  • Share content instantly using simple links

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide a fast, simple, and accessible platform for students, developers, and professionals to store and share their ideas without complexity.

Privacy & Responsibility

Users are responsible for the content they upload. SyncNote does not encourage misuse of the platform and follows strict content guidelines to maintain a safe environment.
